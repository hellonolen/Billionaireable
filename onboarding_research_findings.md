# Onboarding Research Findings - AI Tools 2025

## Source
UserGuiding Blog: "How Top AI Tools Onboard New Users in 2025"
URL: https://userguiding.com/blog/how-top-ai-tools-onboard-new-users

## Key Insights for Billionaire Dashboard

### 1. Signup Flow Best Practices
**What AI tools ask during signup (3-5 questions):**
- Use case (7 out of 10 tools)
- User role (6 out of 10 tools)
- Purpose (5 out of 10 tools)
- Plan choice (4 out of 10 tools)
- Name (3 out of 10 tools)

**Approach:**
- Conversational tone, not a "security checkpoint"
- Short and personal
- Introduces pricing early (shows confidence)
- Personalizes the experience immediately

**Example: Claude**
- Uses conversational tone
- Introduces pricing upfront
- Trusts users to choose what fits them

### 2. ElevenLabs Specific Approach
**"Activation via effort reduction"**
- Heavily uses **templates and defaults**
- Reduces cognitive load
- Users experience less friction since they're less likely to change what's already offered as default
- **Power of defaults principle**: Pre-filled, ready-to-use elements

### 3. First Session UX
**Show, don't tell:**
- Old-school tooltips/checklists fading (only 4 of 10 tools use them)
- Focus on **embedded experiences**:
  - Empty states with CTAs
  - Example prompts
  - Familiar interfaces
- Product teaches itself as you use it
- **Learn-by-doing** principle

**Example: Perplexity**
- Gives example prompts immediately
- Users see value in seconds without reading guides

### 4. Activation Markers
**40% of AI tools give value BEFORE signup**
- On-site prompts to simplify signup
- "Loginless experience"
- Deliver faster value
- Reframes signup as natural next step, not prerequisite

**Example: Replit**
- Lets visitors run prompts on homepage
- See results first
- Signing up becomes obvious next step

### 5. The Activation Path
1. **Setup moment**: First actions needed to experience value
2. **Aha moment**: Experience value for the first time
3. **Habit moment**: Product becomes part of routine

## Application to Billionaire Dashboard

### Recommended Onboarding Flow

**Step 1: Welcome + Primary Focus** (1 question)
- "Welcome to your billionaire dashboard"
- "What matters most to you right now?"
  - [ ] Wealth Management
  - [ ] Health Optimization
  - [ ] Business Performance
  - [ ] Legacy & Impact

**Step 2: Personalize Dashboard** (1 question)
- Based on their answer, show relevant sections
- "Which areas would you like to track?"
  - Wealth: Portfolio, Investments, Net Worth
  - Health: Biomarkers, Fitness, Longevity
  - Business: Revenue, Team, KPIs
  - Legacy: Philanthropy, Family, Impact

**Step 3: Connect First Data Source** (Action)
- "Let's get you started with real data"
- Show 3-4 integration options based on their focus
- Example defaults/templates to reduce friction
- Skip option available

**Step 4: Set First Goal** (Optional)
- Quick goal creation
- Pre-filled templates based on their focus area
- Can skip and do later

### Key Principles to Apply
1. **3-5 questions maximum** - Keep it conversational
2. **Use defaults and templates** - Reduce cognitive load (ElevenLabs approach)
3. **Show value before asking for commitment** - Let them see dashboard first
4. **Personalize immediately** - Use answers to customize what they see
5. **Make it skippable** - But save progress for later
6. **Embedded experience** - No tooltips, let them learn by doing

### Data to Collect
1. Primary focus area (Wealth/Health/Business/Legacy)
2. Which dashboard sections to prioritize
3. First integration preference
4. Optional: Name, timezone, notification preferences

### What to Avoid
- Long forms
- Generic "tours" that don't collect data
- Tooltips and checklists
- Forcing completion before accessing product
- Rainbow colors and cartoonish design
